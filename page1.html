The pyramids of Egypt are massive stone tombs built for pharaohs over 4,000 years ago. The most famous are the three Great Pyramids at Giza, including the Great Pyramid of Khufu, which was the tallest structure in the world for over 3,800 years. Archaeologists believe tens of thousands of workers spent decades cutting and hauling millions of limestone blocks to construct them.
